About Me

My name is Mark Reifke. I graduated from the University of Arizona Full Stack Coding Bootcamp in May of 2021. I currently live in San Tan Valley, AZ. In August of 2018, I went overseas to Kuwait and worked for 2 years as a Force Protection Officer. Within 4 months of working, I got selected and put into a specialty group. While working in that group I got to meet a lot of other contractors that worked within the IT field. While getting to know them and talking with them I started to really get an interest for computer coding and how it all works. I came home in July of 2020 and took some time off to spend with family and just relax from getting home. I started to talk to my brother-in-law who currently works in the computer coding field and found out about these Coding Bootcamps. I knew I wanted to get into some field to do with computers. I've always had a passion for computers and working on them. I just never knew what I really wanted to do with it. That's when I enrolled in the UofA coding bootcamp to take the leap into a new and exciting career field and something I have a passion for.

Then the unfortunate happened and in January of 2021 I tested positive for Covid-19 and had to quit my job at Home Depot as Asset Protection so I could recover from Covid and all the side effects from it. That showed me that I needed to have a career that I could work remotely if needed and be more stable and safe.

Im looking forward to what the future has instore and what I'm going to learn from school and advancing in a career in coding.
